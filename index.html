Welcome to Good Vending, LLC!

We strive hard to provide you, your customers, and your staff with snacks everyone can enjoy.

Good Vending was started in 2020 with one mission: to serve up yummy snacks without the hassle. We take pride in our business and our customers' safety, with extra sanitation during the COVID19 pandemic. This is a small locally-owned business based out of Ypsilanti, Michigan. We always keep in stock a wide variety of snacks to appease children, adults, and health nuts alike! Whether your business is looking for salty, sweet, or hearty, always think Good Vending first! Please be sure to ask about our food allergy accomodations.

How does this all work?

Good Vending, LLC, provides two services. 1) We purchase gently used vending machines! and 2) We provide a full-service vending machine system. You can contact us for either service at the buttons below. For our full service vending, you will provide your business' location, and we will contact you to confirm a day for your contract to begin.

What are the benefits of using Good Vending services?

Good Vending, LLC, provides the vending machine (or can purchase old unused machines from your business). The machines are sanitized regularly and all Good Vending, LLC workers wear face masks and gloves while stocking the machines. All snacks and maintenance on the machine are also maintained by Good Vending, LLC.
